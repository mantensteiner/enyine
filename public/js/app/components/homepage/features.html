Product Details

Dashboard Projects overview.

The dashboard as general starting ground. All your projects, time-critical topics & last project activities. All fully searchable.

Times The projects time & topic history.

Quickly filterable by a powerful full text search. Also helpful is the structured filter by clickable topics and date labels.

Topics & Discussion Topic detail view.

Edit your information fields, set tags & status.
Review spent times & activities.
Discuss info regarding the topic by using comments.


Topics Board Interactive board visualization.

Status based columns with topic-cards to get a top level impression of your project.
Use drag & drop on desktop browsers to move topics between their states.

Editing Efficient inputs.

Quick edit topics and times without leaving the context using overlay dialogs. Use emojis to express your mood.


Inline edit fields directly within lists.

Labels Content classification.

Create custom Labels to classify your Topics.
Truly individualize your Project by setting your own colors & icons!

Notes Project information.

Notes are the right thing for more general information like roadmaps, reports or scratchpads for features.
Notes can be private, which makes them a good alternative for personal todo-lists.
As with all other content fields in enyine, Notes can be written in Markdown.

Search & Filters Find everything, fast.

Quickly find anything by using the global search engine. Use simple terms or a powerful syntax for more specific cases.


Configure & save filters for the topic-lists you often come back to. Addressable by URL for easy bookmarking and sharing.

Manage Project Teams Better together.

Manage your project team as project administrator and invite new members.

Github Integration Your content repository.

The Github Integration reflects your committed work automatically as it creates times and topics.
Helping you to reduce manual inputs by extracting information like your spent hours from the commit message.
No more tedious reconstructing of past time records.

Slack Integration Your information hub.

Many teams, including us, enjoy using the excellent Slack for messaging & document management.
Delegate project-events to a connected Slack-Channel to keep your team informed and have all information in one place.

Activity Log Keeping all actions.

The activity log contains every action that happened, fully searchable. Drill in down to the field level changes.
No responsibility gaps and full transparency to your projects!
